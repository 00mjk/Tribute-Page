Jeffrey Preston Bezos

Founder and CEO of Amazon

Jeff Bezos is an American entrepreneur and investor. He is the founder of Amazon and has a total net worth of $200 billion.

  • Nov 19, 1993 - Jeff Bezos marries MacKenzie Tuttle

  • Dec 19, 1998 - Amazon net sales were at $600 million
